RozKa
A personal time-based habit tracker.
by @siddhartha_b

What it does

Track habits by minutes or as simple yes/no days. Set a time goal if you want one, log each session, and get a next-session nudge scaled to your own history.

Views

  • Momentum: Rises with each session, decays through gaps. A long streak softens the cost of a missed day.
  • Total time: Cumulative hours logged, with goal progress when you’ve set one.
  • Streak: Every unbroken run shown as a column.
  • Sessions / Calendar: Fill glasses scaled to each habit’s own typical day. Gold rim marks a recent best. Tap any day to log.

Storage

Local-first. Your data stays on your device. No account needed.

Free
