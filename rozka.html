RozKa
A personal time-based habit tracker.
by @siddhartha_b
Momentum Total time Streak Calendar Local-first

What it does

Log minutes, not just streaks. Add a habit, set a time goal if you want one, and record each session.

Views

  • Momentum: Rises with each session, decays through gaps. A long streak softens the cost of a missed day.
  • Total time: Cumulative hours logged, with goal progress when you’ve set one.
  • Streak: Every unbroken run shown as a column.
  • Sessions / Calendar: Fill glasses scaled to your best day in a ±7-day window. Tap any day to log minutes.

Storage

Local-first. Your data stays on your device. No account needed.

Your best looks different each day. A perfect streak and a broken one both count. Showing up is the thing. Doing this beats having a perfect plan.
